About WelayDaily

WelayDaily is an independent football (soccer) news site covering match results, transfer news, standings, and fixtures from major leagues and tournaments around the world.

How our content is produced

WelayDaily uses an AI-assisted editorial process. Our system identifies current, publicly reported football news events, then produces original written summaries and analysis in its own words — it does not copy or republish text from other outlets. Featured graphics accompanying our articles are unique, programmatically generated illustrations created specifically for each story; they are stylized graphics, not photographs, and do not depict real individuals.

Standings and fixture data are sourced from public football data providers and updated automatically on a regular schedule.

Our editorial standards

We aim for accuracy and fairness in every story. If you believe something we've published is inaccurate, please get in touch via our Contact page and we'll review it promptly.

Why we're transparent about this

We believe readers deserve to know how content is made. WelayDaily is run as a small, independent operation, and AI tools allow us to cover more of the football world, more often, than a small team could manage alone — while keeping every word and every image original.
